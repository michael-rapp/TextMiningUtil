OptimalStringAlignmentSimilarity()

Optimal string alignment similarity (abbreviated as OSA similarity) is the gain metric pendant to the OptimalStringAlignmentDistance. It measures the percentage of single-character edits (insertions, deletions, or substitutions) and transpositions of adjacent characters that are necessary to change one text into another, among the number of characters of the longer of both texts. OSA similarity always evaluates to heuristic values within the interval 0,1. Texts that evaluate to greater heuristic values are considered to be more similar than texts that evaluate to smaller heuristic values.
