HammingAccuracy()

Hamming accuracy is the gain metric pendant to the HammingDistance. It measures the percentage of characters at corresponding positions that are equal. Hamming accuracy always evaluates to heuristic values within the interval 0,1. Texts that evaluate to greater heuristic values are considered to be more similar than texts that evaluate to smaller heuristic values.
